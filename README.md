## About Me

Commodity operations specialist with hands-on experience in **base and precious metals**, logistics, and compliance across Africa, Europe & Asia.

Currently **Metal Operator at Mercuria Energy Trading** — one of the world's largest independent commodity groups.

- End-to-end physical trade management: contract confirmation → vessel coordination → delivery → invoicing
- Hedging execution and risk assessment alongside trading desks (futures, CFDs, pricing windows)
- KYC / AML / sanctions compliance on counterparties, vessels, and trade flows
- Board member at Debitors Management SA · Pursuing **DAS in Commodity Trading** @ University of Geneva
